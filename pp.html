Information I Collect

I collect the following information to provide and enhance my services:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details you provide during registration or inquiries.
  • Account Information: When you register using my registration form, I may collect additional information such as your username, password, and profile photo.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, I may collect billing information and transaction history through payment processors like Stripe.
  • Usage Data: I may collect information about how you use my services, including your IP address, device type, browser type, and usage patterns to improve service delivery and user experience.
  • Profile Information: You may also provide additional information for your profile, such as a profile photo, which I will store securely.

How I Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain my services.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand my services.
  • To process your transactions and manage your orders.
  • To communicate with you, including sending verification emails, promotional content, and customer support.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure security.

Data Security

I implement various security measures to protect your data, using techniques such as encryption and secure storage. I also employ measures to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, and misuse of your information.

Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, I may share your information with trusted third-party service providers, including payment processors like Stripe, to perform services on my behalf, such as processing payments and managing customer accounts.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • The right to object to or restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time where I rely on your consent to process your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
